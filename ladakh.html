Ladakh, India Tour

Day 1

Arrival at Leh

· On arrival, pickup from the airport and transfer to hotel.
· After welcoming at the hotel, enjoy tea and coffee.
· Later, acclimatize yourself to the low atmospheric pressure because of the high altitude of Ladakh
· In the late afternoon, drive to Shanti Stupa.
· Overnight stay at the hotel.
Day 2

Leh to Likir and Alchi in Sham Valley

· After breakfast, drive to Likir Monastery.
· En route visit Phater Sahib, Magnetic Hill, and the Indus and Zansker Sangam.
· Continue the drive to Alchi Monastery.
· Be back to Leh by evening.
· Overnight stay at the hotel in Leh.
Day 3

Leh to Thiksey and Pangong Lake

· After breakfast, drive to Pangong Lake (the biggest high altitude lake in Asia).
· Enjoy the turquoise color panoramic lake and the magnificent mountains.
· Overnight stay at the camp.
Day 4

Pangong to Leh

· After breakfast, drive back to Leh.
· En route visit Thiksey Monastery and Shey Monastery.
· Overnight stay at the hotel.
Day 5

Leh to Nubra Valley

· After breakfast, drive to Nubra Valley via Khardungla (highest motorable road in the world).
· On arrival, check-in your camp at Hundar.
· In the afternoon, enjoy the beauty of sand dunes.
· Take a ride on the double humped Bactrian camels.
· Overnight stay at the camps.
Day 6

Nubra to Diskit and Sumoor and Back to Leh

· Early morning, visit Diskit Monastery and enjoy a wonderful view of Nubra Valley.
· Later, drive to Sumoor village.
· Continue the drive back to Leh.
· Overnight stay at the hotel.
· next day: After breakfast, drop to Leh airport to catch the onward flight back home.
