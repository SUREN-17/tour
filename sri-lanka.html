    Tour Overview

Experience the Best of Sri Lanka in 5 Nights

Day 1

Colombo to Kandy

· On arrival at Colombo International Airport, proceed to Kandy.
· En-route visit the Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage.
· On arrival at Kandy, check in to the hotel.
· Later proceed for sightseeing at the New Ranweli Spice Garden.
· Explore the various varieties of spices grown in Sri Lanka.
· After the garden visit, spend the evening at leisure.
· Return to the hotel.
· Overnight stay in Kandy.
Day 2

Kandy Sightseeing

· After breakfast, proceed for sightseeing of Kandy.
· Visit the Temple of the Tooth Relic and the Royal Botanical Gardens.
· In the evening, enjoy a cultural show presented by the local artists.
· Return to the hotel.
· Overnight stay in Kandy.
Day 3

Kandy to Bentota

· After breakfast, proceed to Bentota.
· On arrival at Bentota, check in to the hotel.
· Spend the rest of the day at leisure.
· Overnight stay in Bentota.
Day 4

Bentota Sightseeing

· After breakfast, proceed for sightseeing of Bentota.
· Enjoy a boat ride on the Madu River.
· Visit a Sea Turtle Farm and learn about the turtle hatchery process.
· Later, return to the hotel.
· Overnight stay in Bentota.
Day 5

Bentota to Colombo

· After breakfast, proceed to Colombo.
· On arrival in Colombo, check in to the hotel.
· Later, enjoy a city tour of Colombo.
· In the evening, enjoy shopping and nightlife in the city.
· Afterward, return to the hotel.
· Overnight stay in Colombo.
Day 6

Departure

· After breakfast, check out from the hotel and transfer to Colombo International Airport for departure
